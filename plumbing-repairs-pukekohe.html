Plumbing Repairs We handle

Faucet & shower repairs

Faucet leaks are a major headache and a waste of money when compared to a plumbing emergency. The majority of faucet and shower issues start out little with slow dripping or low water pressure which we often don’t pay much attention to. As long as the tap remains unfixed, the problem gets worse. A blocked or leaky shower is more difficult to fix unless you have professional help, whether it's due to an ill-fitting shower tray or a leak in the shower drain. Here at Pukekohe Plumbers, we can assist you in detecting leaky pipes and showers as soon as possible and performing the necessary repairs without causing more harm.

Blocked toilet repairs

Blocked toilets are a common issue in every household that happens frequently due to numerous problems both minor and major. In both cases, you are bound to suffer some sort of inconvenience. Hence, blocked toilets need immediate repairs as they can be a menace if not addressed quickly. Most of the blockages are caused due to flushing of too much toilet paper or other non-flushable items like diapers, sanitary pads, and toys, build-up of sediments, and minerals in the drain pipes. Whatever the cause of the blockage, we can help you clear your clogged toilets and fix them as soon as possible.

Sink Repairs (Kitchen & Bathroom)

Be it in the kitchen or bathroom, sinks are an important part of your household that aids you with cleaning yourself as well as the dishes. Any issue with the sink can make the chores ineffective and frustrating. Sometimes there may be clogging due to the accumulation of food wastes and dirt. As it is a common scenario in every household, some clogging unclogs itself without your supervision. But some issues grow into major ones which require professional assistance to have it checked and fixed. At Pukekohe Plumbers, we repair cracks, blockage, holes, and other damages in the sink with our specialized set of equipment. Our experienced plumbers deliver the service that well-suits your needs.

Gutter Repairs

Gutter systems are the best way to prevent moisture from pooling underneath the house and damaging your roof. They help keep your house dry and free from mold. Due to bad weather conditions like heavy rain or wind, gutters get damaged over time. If you notice any crack or damage in the gutters, get them fixed by professionals to ensure the security of your house. We have the experienced team to fix all kinds of gutter system repairs in a cost-effective manner as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Spouting & Downpipe repairs

If your property's downpipes are blocked, the contents may freeze during the winter months. Frozen water expands, and if it expands sufficiently, it might shatter the downpipe. It may also cause cracks or holes in the downpipe and sometimes fall off the roof during extreme weather conditions. Leaves and branches from the trees nearby also contribute to the clogging of the gutters. As a result, clearing blocked gutters and downpipes as soon as possible is always the wisest move. You will be better off if they are clear and everything is flowing smoothly as usual.

Hot Water cylinder repairs

Hot water cylinders are an integral part of your home that helps in keeping you warm. Any issue in the water heating system can ruin your day and leave you in discomfort. While it may appear that replacing your water heater is the most convenient answer, repairs may prolong the life of your water heater by many years, saving you both time and money. If you notice any strange sounds, leaking water cylinders, or delay in producing hot water you should call for help. Our licensed plumbers evaluates and solves the fundamental causes of defective hot water cylinders since we deal with everything related to plumbing repairs and maintenance services.

Underground pipe leaks

Underground pipe leaks are common yet they don’t catch our attention as fast as any other leaks in your household. You may not notice the leak as it is buried deep down in the ground, but it is quietly wasting water and possibly costing you money. Most of the modern water leak detectors available now are much more sensitive as well as accurate when compared to the older ones. By incorporating modern technologies, we offer reliable services to trace down the leak and resolve it as fast as possible.
