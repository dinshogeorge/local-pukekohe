Hot Water Cylinder Installations & Repairs

The field of hot water cylinders is always expanding with the introduction of new high-end models and advancements in technology. As the sector evolves, a regular plumber might not be able to fix it at all. It is critical to have hot water cylinders checked and repaired by experts at the earliest since they might result in a significant increase in your energy expenses. Therefore, further delaying the repair might result in total system failure, which would be exceedingly costly.

Hot water cylinder repairs in Pukekohe

No matter how small or big the repair is, it is quite beneficial to address it as soon as you identify the issue. If you take care of it early, you may be able to extend the life of your hot water cylinder with small repairs or replacements of its parts. At Pukekohe Plumbers, we offer a wide range of hot water cylinder repairs all across Pukekohe at budget-friendly prices along with high-quality work.

Symptoms of a faulty hot water cylinder

  • Loud noises
  • High power bill
  • Foul smell
  • Leaks in the cylinder
  • Rusty water
  • High or low temperature
  • Delay in producing hot water

Hot water cylinder replacement and installation

Being familiar with all kinds of hot water cylinders from low-end to high-end models, our team of trained and experienced plumbers offers reliable suggestions regarding the replacement and installation of new systems in the place of the existing ones. If your hot water system is more than 10 years old, it is recommended to have it replaced with a new model which offers more efficiency and better performance thereby, saving you money and time.

We provide Hot water cylinder servicing

Even though your hot water system doesn’t show any signs of failing, it is important to have your hot water cylinder checked and serviced once in a while to safeguard your system from any damage. At Pukekohe plumbers, we deal with all kinds of maintenance services including checking the valves and other safety devices of your hot water cylinder to extend the life of the equipment and ensure a safe environment for your family.
