Water leak detection services Pukekohe

Think you’ve got a leak? Worry not, no leak is big or small for us. Our well-trained team of Pukekohe Plumbers has got the right gear and skills to tackle any issue regarding water leakage.

Are you experience these types of leaks ?

Household leaks (bathroom, kitchen)

As leaks in the bathroom and kitchen faucets are a common scenario in many households, homeowners don't understand the seriousness of this water wastage until it develops into a major problem that demands professional supervision. Every household leak should be addressed promptly to resource the risk of water damage.

Underground Water Leaks

Underground pipe leaks are common yet they don’t catch our attention as fast as any other leaks in your household. You may not notice the leak as it is buried deep down in the ground, but it is quietly wasting water and possibly costing you money. If left untreated, it may pose a major threat to the structure of your house.

Water mains leaks

Leaks in the water main are of great concern as it disrupts the whole water flow to your house causing significant wastage of water. Water leakage can be on either your side of the pipe or the city's side. As soon as you sense leakage, you should call our experienced and certified plumbers to trace the source of the leakage if it's on your side of the pipe and get it fixed to avoid further damage to the system.

Signs of water leaks

  • Musty smell
  • Wet patches on the ground
  • Cracks in foundation
  • Water stains on the wall
  • High water bill
  • Dripping sound

How to detect water leaks

The most effective way to detect the water leak is by using leak detectors that identify even the tiniest of leaks in the pipes or other fixtures immediately. Some leak detectors like Electronic leak detectors can be installed which emit an alarm to alert us about the slightest leaks in the system so that you can fix them as soon as it occurs. Monitoring the water bill is also one of the easiest methods to find the leakage in the water system. But it cannot find the exact location of the leak.

Our leak detection method for water leaks

Our team uses the most modern technology of acoustic leak detection that indicates the exact location of the leaks without having to dig the whole pipe segment. The equipment senses vibration or unusual hissing sound that arises due to the pressure difference in water from the leaky pipeline which can be fixed by our skilled and dedicated plumbers in no time.

Underground Water Leak Repairs

We employ a highly advanced kind of acoustic leak detection for underground water leak repairs, which quickly pinpoints the source of leaks. The mobile sensor attached to the device records the sound of water moving through pipes, and any difference in water pressure, such as hissing, helps in locating the leak. Our specialized plumbers locate the leak and excavate the area, replacing the leaking pipe with a new one. Leaking is more likely in older pipelines that are prone to corrosion over a period of time. So we recommend you to change the entire pipeline if it is older than ten years in order to safeguard your water system and home.

Why Choose Us

With minimal disruption, our proficient crew will trace down the source of the water leakage and resolve it at the earliest to prevent further complications to your system. You can rest assured while we get your water system back to functioning.
